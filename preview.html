How Zeparture works

Four steps. Zero sales calls.

Every departure is fixed-date and self-serve — built for travelers who'd rather book in two minutes than sit through a pitch.

01

Pick a departure

Browse fixed dates and a transparent, all-in price — no quote requests, no waiting on a callback.

02

Questions

Frequently asked

How does booking work without a salesperson?

Every departure has a fixed date, fixed price and published itinerary. You review everything online and reserve your seat directly — no quote requests and no calls required to get started.

Can I join as a solo traveler?

Yes — Zeparture is built around solo travelers joining a curated group. Accommodation is on a twin/triple sharing basis, so you'll be paired with fellow travelers from the same batch.

How do I pay, and is it secure?

Seats are reserved with an online deposit through a secure payment gateway. The balance is collected as per the schedule shared at the time of booking.

What if my destination isn't live yet?

Destinations marked "On-request" or "Coming Soon" can be requested directly — tap "Notify Me" or "Request This Trip" and we'll reach out as soon as a batch is being formed.

What's the cancellation & refund policy?

Cancellation and refund terms are shared in full at checkout before you pay your deposit, so there are no surprises — message us on WhatsApp if you'd like to see them ahead of booking.
