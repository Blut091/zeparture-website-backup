How Zeparture works

Built for travelers who'd rather book than be sold to.

Every departure on Zeparture is fixed-date, fixed-price and fully published before you ever say a word to anyone. Here's exactly what happens between you finding a trip and landing with your group.

01

Pick a departure

Every live destination shows a fixed date range, a capped group size, and one all-in price — no "starting from", no quote form. What you see is what you pay.

02

Reserve your seat

Secure your spot with an online deposit through a secure payment gateway. Seats are first-come — once a batch fills, it's closed and the next one opens.

03

Meet your group

You're added to a pre-departure group with the other travelers in your batch and your dedicated trip captain, well before wheels-up — so nobody's a stranger on day one.

04

Travel together

Your trip captain is on-ground for the entire batch — handling check-ins, transfers and the day-to-day so you can actually be on the trip instead of managing it.

Why it's built this way

No salesperson, on purpose.

Price you see is final

No negotiation, no "let me check with my manager." Inclusions and exclusions are listed upfront on every departure page.

Speed over small talk

If you already know you want to go, you shouldn't need a 20-minute call to find out if you can. Reserve in the time it takes to read this page.

Built for solo travelers

Twin/triple sharing batches mean you're never paying a solo-traveler markup, and never traveling truly alone either.
